Welcome

Devin Liu is a composer and producer specialising in music for film, television, and games, as well as modern rock and metal production. With a background in classical training and a deep foundation in contemporary production techniques, Devin blends emotive orchestration with cutting-edge sound design to craft scores that elevate storytelling across media.

In addition to scoring for screen, Devin produces, mixes, and masters for metal bands, creating heavy, polished, and sonically aggressive records. His work draws from both cinematic sensibilities and modern metal aesthetics, making him a versatile collaborator for directors, developers, and musicians alike.
