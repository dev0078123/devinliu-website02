About Devin

Devin Liu is a composer, producer, and performer whose work navigates the space between cinematic storytelling and emotionally immersive sound. Drawing from a background in classical piano and a deep connection to contemporary rock and metal, his music blends orchestral scale with modern sonic aesthetics — often balancing delicacy with intensity, structure with atmosphere.

Currently based in London and studying at Guildhall School of Music & Drama, Devin’s work spans film scoring, game music, and live band performance. He approaches composition as a narrative craft — shaping musical arcs that serve emotion, whether through hybrid scoring techniques or collaborative arrangements in band settings.

Alongside his work in media, Devin is an active member of the bands Pillowman and Ostinato, where he serves as both producer and performer. These projects allow him to explore a more tactile, band-driven process, contributing to sound that draws from post-metal, progressive, and cinematic influences. Across both live and studio contexts, his focus remains on creating music that resonates with weight, intention, and movement.

Devin’s work also includes collaborations with independent artists and instrumentalists, providing arrangement, mixing, and compositional support across a range of genres. Whether working to picture, developing a concept EP, or shaping a moment in a live set, he’s drawn to music that builds a world around the listener — with narrative at its core.
